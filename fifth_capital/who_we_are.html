Our Mission and Our Investment Philosophy

About Fifth Capital

Fifth Capital Management, LLC, is a Registered Investment Advisor (RIA). At Fifth Capital, we manage two separate strategies using separately managed accounts:

  1. Enterprising Strategy. This is a value investing strategy consisting of:
    • Quantitatively Cheap Stocks (This is the foundation of our strategy. Buying cheap stocks works. We always keep a basket of cheap stocks which have been proven to provide significant outperformance over time)
    • Compounders (Good companies at attractive prices that are growing their sales, earnings, and book values over time)
  2. Defensive Strategy. We look for stable companies at attractive prices that are growing their dividends over time.

Both strategies are designed to protect capital first, and compound capital second. We live by Warren Buffett’s famous 2 rules:

  • Rule #1: Don’t Lose Money
  • Rule #2: Don’t Forget Rule #1

We’ve spent years studying the markets in depth. During the course of our studies, we have found certain things that work, and many things that don’t. Value investing is one thing that works consistently over long periods of time. It is one of the most logical and persistent anomalies in markets. Although not always easy to execute, it is an extremely simple concept: Buy assets for less than their intrinsic value. We are looking to buy the proverbial $1 for 50 cents, and we know that by using this margin of safety, we provide ourselves the best chance of protecting capital while simultaneously producing superior investment returns over time.

About Our Founder

Chris Caballero is specialized in portfolio management, fixed income, option strategies, and security valuation. In his current role, Chris will manage the strategic direction of the firm and provide trade allocations for the firm's clients. He completed studies in finance at Southern Methodist University, and at the acclaimed Columbia University in New York City. Chris acquired his expertise as the lead portfolio manager at Cambrick Capital Partners. Morgan Stanley Wealth Management took notice of his early success and invited him to join in 2010. There, he worked in the Wealth Management division. He holds a FINRA Series 3, 63 and 65 securities license and is also pursuing his charter in the CFA program. Chris grew up in Southern California and now resides in Dallas Texas.
