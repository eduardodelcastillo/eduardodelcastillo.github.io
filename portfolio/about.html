About Eduardo

My name is Eduardo, a designer based in Adelaide, Australia.


Originally trained in engineering and IT, I switched to design to combine my passion for art and technology. I believe that most of this world's problems can be solved by good design. Furthermore, design can inspire awe and wonder challenging our bias of what's possible!


When I'm not working, I'm usually traveling and exploring new places. But if there's something you need (like an eye for good design or UX, visual or web projects) or just want to say hi, don't hesitate to reach out to me!
